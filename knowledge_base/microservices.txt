## invoice-service
Description: Provides invoice details for customer orders.
Endpoint: GET /api/invoice?orderId={id}

## shipment-service
Description: Tracks shipment status for an order.
Endpoint: GET /api/track?orderId={id}

## returns-service
Description: Handles product returns within 7 days of delivery.
Endpoint: POST /api/return
Parameters: orderId, reason

## account-service
Description: Fetches customer account details.
Endpoint: GET /api/account?customerId={id}
